Chicken Sandwich

Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Tony Cajuns Seasoning
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Hamburger Buns

    • Directions:

    1. Tenderize the chicken by using a tenderizer to pound the chicken til quarter of an inch flat.
    2. Season chicken generously.
    3. Turn stove up to medium heat.
    4. Set chicken on pan. Allow it to sear for 4-5 minutes.
    5. Flip and repeat for opposit side.
