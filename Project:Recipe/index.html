Bake Chicken Recipes

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Tony Cajuns Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Set oven to 350 Farenheit.
  2. Generously season chicken with all seasonings.
  3. Place chicken thighs in large tray.
  4. Pour small amount of olive oil over chicken.
  5. Place chicken in oven and let cook for 30 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice and enjoy!
